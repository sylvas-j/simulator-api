Smart Home Monitoring Simulator is built on a model trained with different ambient signal datasets.
The dataset consist of three features ('numDistinctSensors','sensorCount-Chair','sensorCount-DiningRoom', 'sensorCount-Hall','sensorCount-Office','sensorCount-WorkArea','sensorElTime-Chair','sensorElTime-DiningRoom', 'sensorElTime-Hall','sensorElTime-Office', 'sensorElTime-WorkArea','lastSensorLocation','lastMotionLocation', 'sensorElTime-OutsideDoor', 'lastSensorEventSeconds','activity').
To used the simulator:

  1. Download the test data from Smart Home > Download Records
  2. Upload the downloaded data to the system and wait for your result.
